Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2024-03-28

Introduction

zDataStorage.com ("we", "us") respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains the way we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you access our app hosted on the Google Cloud Platform ("App").

Information We Collect

Information you provide:
Account details: Email address, name, [other relevant registration info]
User-generated content: Any data you input while using the App (e.g., text, images, files, etc.)
Automatically collected information:
Usage data: Details on how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, pages visited, timestamps)
Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
Location data: Approximate location (if you grant permission)
Cookies and similar technologies: Small data files to track user behavior, preferences, and improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

Provide and improve the App: Process information to deliver core functionality, enhance features, and personalize your experience.
Security: Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security risks.
Customer support: Respond to your inquiries and provide troubleshooting.
Analytics: Understand how our App is used to make informed development decisions.
Communications: Send updates, notices, or other relevant information about the App (you can manage preferences).
Compliance with law: If legally compelled to do so.

Information Sharing

Service providers: With third parties who assist us with operations (e.g., storage, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
Business transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred.
With your consent: If we seek to share data outside these instances, your explicit permission will be obtained.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data on Google Cloud:

Encryption: Your data may be encrypted in transit and at rest.
Access controls: We restrict access to authorized personnel only.
Monitoring: We actively monitor for security risks.
Google Cloud security: We leverage the robust security infrastructure provided by Google Cloud.
Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. You can request data deletion (subject to certain limitations).

Your Rights

You may have the following rights, depending on your jurisdiction:

Access and portability: Request a copy of your personal data.
Rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
Deletion: Request your data to be erased.
Restriction of processing: Limit how we use your data.
Object to processing: Object to certain uses of your data.
Withdrawing consent: If we rely on your consent, you may withdraw it.
Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. If there are significant changes, we'll notify you via email or a prominent notice within the App.
