Misappropriation

Civil Remedies Under Illinois Law

Recovery of Losses

The injured partner can sue for the return of embezzled funds or compensation for misappropriated assets.

Dissolution of Partnership

Misconduct of this nature can be grounds for seeking dissolution of the partnership under the Illinois Uniform Partnership Act (765 ILCS 206/), which governs partnerships in Illinois.

Damages for Breach of Fiduciary Duty

Partners owe each other fiduciary duties, including the duty of loyalty and the duty of care. Misappropriation or embezzlement can be grounds for a lawsuit for breach of these fiduciary duties.
